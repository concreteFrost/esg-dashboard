The Challenge:

ESG is no longer a nice to have, it is a fundamental pillar of doing business in the twenty-first century. In order to generate shared and sustained value, companies need to better understand the impact of their operations and better engage with stakeholders. However, the ESG landscape is evolving quickly and it can be a challenge to understand where to start, how to collect data and what it means.
